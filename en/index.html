Frequently asked questions

Questions and answers

What is ProntoLoc?

ProntoLoc is a 100% digital platform to rent and list tools, equipment and properties, connecting those who have with those who need, without red tape.

What can I rent on ProntoLoc?

From construction equipment — such as concrete mixers, scaffolding, trailers and tools — to properties, spaces and virtually any asset that can be rented.

How does renting work?

You find the item in the catalog, book the period on the calendar, arrange the details via chat and sign the digital contract. Everything tracked on the web or the app.

Is the contract digital?

Yes. The contract is generated as a PDF and signed electronically by the parties, with an audit trail for each signature.

Who sets the rental price?

The price and the period (hourly, daily, weekly or monthly) are set by each item's own advertiser.

How much does it cost to list?

You start free on the free plan. For those who need more, the Premium plan costs R$ 49.99 per month.

Does ProntoLoc have an app?

The platform is already available on the web and the native app for iOS and Android is on the way. Join the VIP list to be notified at launch.
